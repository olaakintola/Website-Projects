Fitness Regime Plan

VIP Regime Plan

This is also known as our Gold standard plan
This involves having your own personal fitness instructor that will work with you one-on-one through a customised fitness plan that is most convenient for you
Prior to the commencement of the classes, you will have a one hour chat with two of our instructors to ensure that they work out a fitness plan that bests suits your needs.
This service is available 7 days a week
This service cost 50 euro a month

Standard Regime Plan

This plan involves being a class of at most 5 individuals with the same fitness needs to ensure that attention is being given to the attendees even though it is a group session
This session is available 5 days and can be accessed only in the evenings
Weekends are not included
Even though this is a group session, we ensure a personal touch by having a one hour chatwith one of our instructors before you join the gym to ensure we match you with a class that best suits you need
This service cost 30 euro a month

Introductory Taster Plan

This is an entirely free service one month plan
There is no commitment to join the gym at the end of the plan
The class size is up to a maximum of 30 individuals
The service can be accessed only during week days
We hope to give our best during this one month that you get to spend with us to ensure that you not only commit to stay with us but also recommend us to family and friends
